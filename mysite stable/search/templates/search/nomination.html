Nomination

The association will publish a journal called Indian Journal of Plant Protection, and shall be published six monthly or two issues in a year. It shall include articles, notes or news giving results based on field experimentations or work done of adoptive nature.There will be responsible for the publication of the journal regularly.
The Editor-in-chief shall be the publisher of the journal also.The annual subscription for the jounal for the non-members shall be Rs.250/-. Advertisement charges in the journal are Rs. 1000/- per page and Rs. 750/- for half a page for one issue. The cost for printing an advertisement on the outer back cover is Rs.2500/- and inner back cover is Rs. 1500/- for each issue of the journal.
